Audit And Accounting

Internal Auditing(IA) is an objective assurance and consulting activity formed to add value to an organisation’s operations. It helps achieve strategic objectives for improving risk management, control, and governance processes’ effectiveness. With a commitment to integrity and accountability, our internal auditing services provide much more value to senior management and governing bodies with an objective source of independent advice.

​ Auditing is classified into different types and levels on the basis of how auditing is performed according to the scopes, purposes, and procedures. At the London Strategy and Consulting Group, internal auditors help in addressing Risk, Regulatory, and Compliance challenges. We make sure our clients are provided with better value for money and an increased assurance. Businesses have large groups of stakeholders for companies who have interest vested in the success of the enterprise. Accounting is the language of business. We at LS&CG make sure that our accounting involves tracking, reporting, and analysing financial transactions for any business that has economic resources. We provide information for not-for-profit organisations as well. For us, accounting means identifying, measure, and communicate economic information to allow informed decisions by the user.

​ We indulge in successful audit and accounting practices help in improving the method of working and producing goods. At LS&CG, we ensure that the best accounting services for business firms are provided, which will help clients make informed judgments pertaining to the entire company.
